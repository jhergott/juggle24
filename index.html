Welcome to Juggle 24, the most addicting game in the App Store today!

What is Juggle 24?

In Juggle 24 the player starts with 3 balls. The goal is to juggle the balls and not let any of them touch the bottom of the screen. Every 8 seconds a new ball will appear so be ready and see how many balls you can juggle! Very few people can get to 24 balls... do you have what it takes?

How to Play?

  • TAP a ball to juggle and keep it afloat!
  • SWIPE UP on the screen to use a gravity flip!
